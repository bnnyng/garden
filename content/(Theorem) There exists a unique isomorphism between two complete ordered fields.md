> [!abstract] Theorem
> If $\mathbb F_0$ and $\mathbb F_1$ are [[Completeness|complete ordered fields]], then there exists a *unique* [[Isomorphism|isomorphism]] $f: \mathbb F_0 \to \mathbb F_1$.
